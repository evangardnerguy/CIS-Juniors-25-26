Sonic.EXE: Outcome Memories, or just OM for short, is an asymettrical horror game released in August 2025. It currently is in early development with it's first big update around the corner. It features Sonic, Tails, Knuckles, Amy, Dr. Eggman, Metal Sonic and Cream up against a supposed god that goes by X. He is a "faker" of Sonic, taking upon his looks. In an infinite time loop, the fun never ends.
