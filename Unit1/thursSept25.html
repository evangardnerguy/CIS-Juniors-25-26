Basic Text Formatting

Use the strong tag or the b tag to make something bold

The Difference Between Strong and B

The Strong tag is a HTML Semantic tag. There is a meaning behind using the Strong tag. It tells the browser that there is something important about the text inside that tag.

The B tag, means nothing. Its simple bold text.

The em tag and the i tag

The em tag and the i tag make the text inside of them italic. The difference is that em is a semantic tag. The i tag just makes text italic.

The u tag

The u tag simply underlines the text.
